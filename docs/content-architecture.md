# Awtad Energy Sitemap and Content Architecture

## Overview
This content architecture is designed for an Oman-based renewable energy company positioned as a premium infrastructure partner rather than a generic “green solutions” provider. The structure is built to support credibility with industrial operators, utilities, developers, public-sector stakeholders and long-horizon investors.

The content logic follows a deliberate sequence:
1. Establish authority and regional relevance.
2. Clarify solution platforms and service depth.
3. Show industry applicability.
4. Demonstrate project thinking through use cases.
5. Reinforce seriousness through sustainability and insight content.
6. Convert high-intent visitors through a structured contact path.

## Full Sitemap

### Primary Navigation
| Page | Role |
| --- | --- |
| Home | Brand thesis, credibility and first-layer conversion |
| About | Company positioning, operating philosophy and leadership stance |
| Solutions | Technology and service platforms |
| Industries | Sector-specific relevance and buyer alignment |
| Projects / Use Cases | Representative deployment models and program thinking |
| Sustainability / Insights | Thought leadership, ESG logic and long-term value narrative |

## Global UX and Content Notes

### Content Principles
- Use short, high-authority headlines.
- Keep subheadlines descriptive, not inflated.
- Tie supporting points to commercial and operational value.
- Avoid generic phrases like “leading provider,” “innovative solutions,” or “powering a sustainable future.”
- Favor clear nouns and verbs: operate, deploy, integrate, maintain, coordinate, evaluate, strengthen.

### Conversion Principles
- Use “Start a Project Brief” as the dominant CTA language across the site.
- Let secondary CTAs route into page-specific exploration.
- Keep form-driven conversion tied to quality, not volume.

### Image Principles
- Use large editorial or cinematic imagery.
- Favor Oman/Gulf-relevant geography and infrastructure environments.
- Avoid stock clichés: handshakes, boardrooms, smiling worker closeups, generic green fields.

### Structural Principles
- Each page should balance trust, clarity and visual authority.
- Solutions and industries should be easy to scan.
- Project scenarios should feel plausible, not exaggerated.
- Sustainability content should strengthen commercial credibility, not read like a separate CSR brochure.
